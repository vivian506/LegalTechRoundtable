About the Event

Where Legal Leaders
Talk Frankly About What's Next

AI, contract automation, and shifting compliance demands are changing the legal landscape faster than most teams can track. This forum exists to cut through the noise.

The LegalTech Executive Roundtable brings together 60–80 General Counsel, Heads of Legal, Legal Operations Directors, and Legal Innovation Leaders for a single day of structured peer discussion across 5 specialist tracks.

25 Closed-Door Roundtables

Groups of 10–15 executives, each session facilitated by an experienced legal or technology leader. Chatham House rules.

Personalised Agenda

You select which sessions to attend in advance. Every hour of your day is relevant to your priorities.

Curated 1-on-1 Meetings

Multiple structured networking slots throughout the day — matched to your current challenges and interests.

Canapé & Drinks Reception

The day closes with an evening reception — the ideal setting to continue conversations from the room.

Discussion Tracks

AI, Automation & Smart Workflows

Use Cases for GenAI in Contract Drafting & Review

Where is AI genuinely saving time, and where does human oversight remain non-negotiable?

Automating NDAs, MSAs & Routine Legal Documents

Practical approaches to document automation that actually get adopted across the business.

AI in Legal Research — Value or Hype?

Cutting through the noise on AI research tools: what delivers ROI and what doesn't.

Risks & Governance in Deploying AI in Legal Teams

Building the right guardrails before you scale AI across your legal function.

Building a Business Case for LegalTech Investment

How to get board and C-suite sign-off for technology spend in a cost-conscious environment.

Legal Operations & Transformation

LegalOps Maturity Models — Where Are You Now?

Benchmarking your legal operations function and identifying the highest-impact next steps.

Managing Legal Spend & Driving Cost Transparency

Tools and processes that bring clarity to external and internal legal costs.

Selecting the Right LegalTech Stack for Your Team

How to evaluate, procure, and implement technology without creating new problems.

Collaborating with Procurement, Compliance & Finance

Breaking down silos to deliver better outcomes for the business.

Change Management: Driving LegalTech Adoption Internally

Why technology fails without people strategy — and what good adoption looks like.

Risk, Compliance & ESG

ESG Compliance Tracking Tools — What Works

Separating the genuinely useful from the box-ticking: practical ESG compliance approaches.

Data Privacy & Cross-Border Compliance Challenges

Navigating an increasingly fragmented global data privacy landscape.

Anti-Money Laundering & KYC Automation

Where technology is accelerating AML and KYC processes — and the risks it introduces.

Managing Regulatory Change at Scale

Systems and structures for keeping pace with a rapidly evolving regulatory environment.

Contract Risk Assessment & Mitigation Using Tech

Using technology to identify, flag, and manage risk at the contract level.

Technology & Innovation in Law Firms

Billing, Timekeeping & CRM Tools — What's Worth the Investment?

An honest review of the tools law firms are actually getting value from.

Tech-Enabled Client Collaboration in Law Firms

How technology is reshaping the client relationship and service delivery model.

How Mid-Size Firms Can Compete with BigLaw Using Technology

Levelling the playing field through smart technology adoption and differentiation.

LPM (Legal Project Management) in a Tech-Supported Practice

Embedding LPM disciplines with the support of the right tools and processes.

Automating Litigation Support & Discovery

The current state of litigation tech and where automation is delivering real efficiency.

Data, Security & Governance

Cyber Risk, Data Breaches & the Role of Legal

How legal teams are taking greater ownership of cyber risk strategy and incident response.

AI Auditability & Legal Implications of Black-Box Tools

What happens when you can't explain how an AI decision was made — and legal gets the call.

Governing Data Ownership Across Teams & Borders

Practical frameworks for managing data rights and responsibilities in complex organisations.

Managing Data for Litigation, Investigations & eDiscovery

Best practices for data preservation, collection, and review in a tech-enabled environment.

Shadow IT & Legal's Responsibility in the Cloud Era
